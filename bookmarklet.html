The venue code is baked into the bookmark — update it each night by editing the bookmark URL and changing the code.
Step 3
How to use it on the night
1. Open hitthathit.com and start your playlist as normal
2. Open your Musical Bingo host dashboard in another tab
3. Set up the session and open registration
4. Tap your "Send to Musical Bingo" bookmark once
5. A small status bar appears at the top of the hitthathit page
6. It checks every 10 seconds — when the song changes it broadcasts automatically
7. That's it — hands off for the rest of the night
Make sure your host session is open before tapping the bookmark, otherwise songs will have nowhere to broadcast to.
